Braves Legends

These six icons helped shape Braves history through record-breaking performances, unmatched leadership, and lifelong dedication to the team.

Hank Aaron

#44 – Outfielder
All-time home run leader (755), civil rights pioneer, and Braves icon.

Chipper Jones

#10 – Third Baseman
Hall of Famer, 1999 NL MVP, played entire career with the Braves.

Greg Maddux

#31 – Pitcher
4x Cy Young winner, master of control, and cornerstone of '90s Braves dominance.

Tom Glavine

#47 – Pitcher
2x Cy Young winner, World Series MVP in 1995, Hall of Famer.

John Smoltz

#29 – Pitcher
Dominated as both starter and closer. Cy Young, WS champ, HOF.

Bobby Cox

#6 – Manager
2,500+ wins, 1995 World Series champion, leader of 14 straight division titles.
